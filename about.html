About Our Church

Our Story

Grace Community Church was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: to create a welcoming space where people could experience God's love and grow in their faith journey. What started as a small gathering of 25 people has grown into a vibrant community of over 500 members.

Over the years, we've seen countless lives transformed through the power of God's grace. Our church has been a beacon of hope in our community, providing spiritual guidance, support, and practical help to those in need.

Today, we continue to build on the strong foundation laid by our founders, embracing both tradition and innovation as we serve God and our community.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do

Our Mission

To share God's love, build authentic community, and inspire spiritual growth through worship, discipleship, and service to others.

Our Vision

To be a transformative force in our community, leading people to Christ and equipping them to live out their faith in meaningful ways.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our community

Love

We believe in showing God's love to everyone, creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where all feel valued and accepted.

Community

We foster authentic relationships and support one another in our faith journey, building a strong and caring church family.

Growth

We encourage spiritual growth through biblical teaching, prayer, and discipleship, helping each person develop a deeper relationship with God.

Service

We actively serve our community and world, demonstrating God's love through practical acts of kindness and compassion.

Integrity

We live with honesty and transparency, maintaining high ethical standards in all our actions and relationships.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, honoring God with our best efforts in worship, ministry, and service.
